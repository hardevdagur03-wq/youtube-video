A Decade of Commitment to Quality Education

Matrix, recognized since 2014, has made a name for itself in competitive exam preparation, primarily known for and specialized in JEE Main & Advanced, NEET, NTSE, KVPY, and international Olympiads like RMO and IJSO. As one of the leading and reputed institutes in Rajasthan, Matrix has been known and consistent with delivering outstanding results, with 6000+ students qualifying for JEE Advanced since 2015. Our alumni are progressing in leading departments like Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at IITs such as IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, and IIT Kanpur. In the medical field, 1100+ students have secured admissions in reputed government medical colleges since the beginning of NEET coaching in 2022.


At Matrix, we ensure and commit to provide a life changing and unique learning experience by providing a blended advanced, modern and innovative technology with expert faculty guidance. We focus on developing analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a strong understanding of concepts to ensure a well rounded development. With a reliable and proven history of performance, personalized care, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Matrix wishes and continues to motivate, inspire and support students to achieve their dreams and secure top ranks in the most competitive exams in India.
